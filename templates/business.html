Cbi: Banks, CBI spar over nod for action on fraud loans - Times of India

MUMBAI: Are all frauds criminal in nature? Should bank lending be completely rule-bound and objective? Bankers and CBI officials have different takes. In a meeting of bankers, CBI and Central Vigilance Commission (CVC) last week, the probe agency again raised its long-standing complaint: Lenders are not giving permission to act in cases of fraudulent loans. Bankers say that CBI has the power to call for information and don't require permission to investigate. The agency, however, is of the view that banks are protecting their employees and preventing the recovery of public money lost due to fraud.

First Made-in-India Hero-Harley Motorcycle Officially Revealed

Harley-Davidson recently revealed the X440, a new motorcycle that is made in India. The X440 is powered by a 440cc single-cylinder engine and is expected to be priced under Rs 3 lakh (ex-showroom). The X440 has a neo-retro styling with a round headlight, teardrop-shaped fuel tank, and a single-piece seat. It has a tubular frame and is suspended on an upside-down fork and preload-adjustable twin shock absorbers. Braking is handled by a single disc brake at both ends, with dual-channel ABS as standard.
